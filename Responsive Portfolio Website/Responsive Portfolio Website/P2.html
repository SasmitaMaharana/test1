Starrynight

In this mesmerizing painting titled "With&Without," I captured the essence of duality and transformation through a captivating interplay of contrasting elements and silence of character. The canvas seems to breathe with life as it unveils a profound narrative that revolves around the concept of presence and absence with can be seen in expression of character, and the transformative power they wield.
